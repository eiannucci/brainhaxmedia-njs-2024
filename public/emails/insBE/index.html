Bronchiectasis flares may feel like a big deal. And they can be.
Hi, %%First Name%%!
If you’re experiencing worsening bronchiectasis (BE) symptoms, it could be a sign of a BE flare. BE flares can be unpredictable, hard to recognize, and they can lead to additional damage in your lungs.
That’s why it’s important to let your doctor know when your symptoms worsen. This information can help your doctor determine how to best manage and treat your condition.
If BE symptoms worsen, it could be a BE flare. Some worsening symptoms may include:
Coughing
Mucus production
Change in mucus
thickness or color
Blood in mucus
Shortness of breath
Fatigue
